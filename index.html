Roblox Italian Brainrot Characters

Ballerina Cappuccina

Origin: TikTok meme

Backstory: A graceful ballerina with a cappuccino for a head. She is known for her dramatic and elegant dance moves and is married to Cappuccino Assassino.

Value: Legendary

Cappuccino Assassino

Origin: TikTok meme

Backstory: A deadly assassin with a cappuccino head and knives for limbs. Fueled by caffeine and a thirst for revenge against Bombardino Crocodilo.

Value: Epic

Tralalero Tralala

Origin: TikTok meme

Backstory: A three-legged shark in Nike sneakers who loves Fortnite and has baby shark sons called the Tralalaleritos. He is considered a beach icon and meme monarch.

Value: Rare

Bombardino Crocodilo

Origin: TikTok meme

Backstory: A crocodile with the body of a WWII bomber plane. He has a heated rivalry with Tralalero Tralala.

Value: Mythic

Chimpanzini Bananini

Origin: TikTok meme

Backstory: A green chimpanzee hiding inside a banana peel. When the peel is removed, he becomes muscular.

Value: Legendary

Lirilì Larilà

Origin: TikTok meme

Backstory: A cactus-wearing elephant in sandals who avoids fights unless absolutely necessary. Can stop time and transform into a weapon.

Value: Common

Bombombini Gusini

Origin: TikTok meme

Backstory: A goose-bomber hybrid, brother to Bombardino. He bombs for fun and speaks with a "z" Italian accent.

Value: Mythic

Tung Tung Tung Sahur

Origin: Indonesian Meme

Backstory: A wooden bat-wielding ghost with a scary Thomas the Tank Engine face. He appears if you ignore him three times.

Value: Rare

Brr Brr Patapim

Origin: TikTok meme

Backstory: A massive proboscis monkey-tree hybrid with huge feet.

Value: Epic

Trippi Troppi

Origin: TikTok meme

Backstory: Often depicted as a cat with a shrimp's body, though some videos use the name to describe an obese bear with the head of a fish.

Value: Rare
